# FYP

The application is an AI-powered customer churn prediction and retention optimisation platform designed for telecommunications operators seeking to reduce revenue loss from customer attrition. The platform integrates machine learning churn prediction, per-customer financial quantification, and budget-constrained retention strategy optimisation into a single, accessible dashboard — transforming raw customer data into actionable, financially justified intervention decisions without requiring machine learning expertise from the end user.

The global telecom industry loses an estimated USD 394 billion annually to customer churn, yet the majority of existing churn management tools deliver only probabilistic risk scores, leaving retention managers without guidance on who is worth intervening with or how to allocate a limited budget across competing customer segments. The application addresses this gap directly: every customer record receives not only a churn probability score but a Value at Risk figure — the expected revenue loss weighted by churn probability and Customer Lifetime Value — that ranks customers by financial exposure and drives a scenario-driven budget allocation engine that maximises retained revenue within a defined spend constraint.

The platform is built on an open-source Python stack, deployable as a web application without infrastructure overhead, and designed for adoption by mid-to-large telecom operators, managed service providers, and telecommunications consultancies. A SaaS subscription model targeting regional telecom operators, with an enterprise licensing tier for large-scale deployments, projects profitability within 18 months of commercial launch at a conservative customer acquisition rate.
